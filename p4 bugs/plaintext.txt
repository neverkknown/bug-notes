Server-Side Credentials Storage -- Plaintext

Steps:
Go to xyz.com and create an account
Go to account settings
Enable 2FA
Logout from the account
Re-login using credentials and it will redirect to 2FA page
Press right click and click on view page source
Search email/password 

Credentials will be visible in plaintext
